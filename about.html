About EUSA

We co-build startups with the founders who will shape the next decade.

EUSA is a venture studio based in Barcelona and Miami. We partner with early-stage technical founders to help them raise capital, build commercial traction, and expand into global markets.

Our mission

To increase the success rate of founders by giving them the storytelling, GTM, and investor access needed to raise quickly and scale globally.
