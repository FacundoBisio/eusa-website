For Founders

Build, Launch & Raise With a Venture Studio

We partner with founders who need support on fundraising, GTM, and operations – acting as embedded co-founders focused on capital and traction.

We work with startups that are:
  • Pre-seed.
  • Have a POC, MVP, or prototype.
  • Solving a real, high-urgency problem.
  • Showing early traction signals (early users and pilots).
  • Full-time or committed to going full-time.
  • Coachable, driven, and fast learners.
  • Open to equity partnership.
  • Targeting US/EU markets.
