The Sprint

A 12-week sprint from proof to investor-ready.

We start with a 12-week sprint that helps founders move from firefighting and rejection to running a focused, investor-ready startup.

  • Build proof investors believe in.
  • Clarify your story, traction, and metrics.
  • Raise confidently with the right capital partners.

We help you raise €250k-1M and secure your first customers.

During the 12-week sprint, you get:
  • Investor readiness · A clear, data-backed narrative, refined pitch, and all materials ready for due diligence.
  • Market traction · GTM playbook, validated ICP, and first customer wins that prove repeatability.
  • Financial clarity · Robust financial model, defined KPIs, and an investor-ready data room.
  • Demo Day & capital access · Present to curated EU & US investors, with warm introductions that turn into real conversations.
  • Post-sprint momentum · Continued mentorship and operational support after the sprint concludes.
